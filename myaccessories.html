Necessary Accessories

Shoes

Your shoes are important for gripping the ground. If your feet slip you could hit a bad shot. Ensure that you have proper footwear for your golf game. Style is equally important. If you look good, you play good. You play good, they pay good.

Gloves

Your glove will allow you to grip the club properly, it will also protect your hand from getting blistered.

Balls

Selecting the correct ball is essential. More expensive= softer feel, but that doesnt necessarily mean better. Your best bet is to try them all and make your own decision.
